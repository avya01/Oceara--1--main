Welcome to Oceara

Built for you with love and passion

Who are we?

“We are Oceara, the brainchild of a teenage ocean lover from Mauritius, you are invited to travel the depths of the unknown and discover the world beneath the sea surface. Our website seeks to demonstrate the uniqueness, scope, and significance of the ocean environment. Its vivid species and life forms, balance of which is essential to life on the planet, make it so complex and intriguing. You may be simply interested, a marine lover or even work for ocean protection, join with us as we learn new things, see amazing pictures and help protect the resources for the use by the future generations.”

Our mission

“People’s ocean is all about instigating global consciousness, imparting education and delivering a call to action to conserve and protect the ocean. We plan this by providing a platform allowing people, organizations, and experts to inform, motivate and practice conservation. By using the mesmerizing images of aquatic environments, their variety and significance, we are able to reach out to communities and future generations enabling them to take care of the oceans for a better healthier planet.”
